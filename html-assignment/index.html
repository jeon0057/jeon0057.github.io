About Me.

I am an Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College, currently living in Ottawa. I was born in Seoul, South Korea, on the 24th of Janurary, 2001. When I was three years old my family and I moved to Canada. Some of my favourite pastimes are solving puzzles, drawing, and listening to music. When it comes to music, I tend to listen to any song that I find touching or catchy in a variety of genres, depending on my mood. One sad fact about me is I love cats, but I am allergic to them.

I previously studied architecture at Carleton University, but I found myself wanting to explore different options before spending 4+ years completing my degree. During the pandemic, I started learning some HTML and decided to take IMD. My goal for the future is to start a career in web development and develop other design skills enough to do freelance work on the side.
